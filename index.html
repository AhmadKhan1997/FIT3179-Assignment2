Road Accidents In India

This interactive dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of road safety across India's diverse states. Explore the data to see how car accidents varies across different states in india. In this dashboard different charts visulize different information, such as deaths per 10,000 vechiles, number of deaths each state and also features a interactive map where it displays number of deaths for each state.

The map shows the total number of road accidents from 2016 to 2019 in each state of India. The red areas are the states with the highest number of road accidents, while the blue areas are the states with the lowest number of road accidents. The top five states with the highest number of road accidents are:

  • Tamil Nadu
  • Madhya Pradesh
  • Karnataka
  • Uttar Pradesh
  • Maharashtra

Select the state where you want to center the map, and you can zoom in and out using the slider.

The bar chart shows the number of road accidents in India from 2016 to 2019. Overall from the chart we can interpret that the number of road accidents has been increasing over the years The top three states with the highest number of road accidents in 2019 were Tamil Nadu,Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh. These states accounted for over 30% of all road accidents in India.

The line chart shows the number of road accidents per 10,000 vehicles on a yearly basis in India from 2016 to 2021. The number of road accidents per 10,000 vehicles has been decreasing over the years, from 28.5 in 2016 to 1.3 in 2019 for ANdhra Pradesh and it is similar for almost all other states as well This suggests that while the number of road accidents has been increasing overall, the rate of road accidents per vehicle has been decreasing. This is likely due to a number of factors, including improved road infrastructure, stricter enforcement of traffic laws, and increased awareness of road safety.
